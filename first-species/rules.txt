* All notes have equal length
* All harmonies consist of consonances
* Beginning and end bars must consist of perfect consonances
* There should be more imperfect than perfect consonances
* Next to last bar:
  - Major 6th if CF is in the lower part
  - Minor 3rd if CF is in the upper part
* The counterpoint must be in the same mode as the cantus firmus (in particular,
  if the cp is in the base, cannot start with a fifth interval)
* No _battuta_ octaves (10th into an octave)
